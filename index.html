Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about setting up Highlight Pro, saving your clippings locally, and using the extension completely offline.

Is this extension really 100% free?

Yes! It is completely free to install and use. No credit card, no sign-ups, and no hidden subscriptions. We want to get this into the hands of as many people as possible, so please share it if you find it helpful.

Where are my highlights saved?

All your highlights are saved entirely inside your browser's local storage. This means your data is 100% private, offline, and secure. We do not track, collect, or upload any of your web clippings.

How do I highlight text on a webpage?

Once you install the extension, simply select any text on any webpage. A small color selector overlay will appear instantly. Click your favorite highlight color, and the selection is saved.

Can I right-click to access the highlight menu?

Yes. Select any text, right-click, and choose "Highlight Pro" from the browser's context menu to pick a color or apply a strikethrough — no need to open the panel at all.

Can the page actually auto-scroll to a highlight?

Yes. Every highlight you create adds a colored tag to the scrollbar — each one labeled with the first few words of that highlight. Click any tag and the page jumps straight to it.

Does it work on PDFs or local files?

Yes! By enabling "Allow access to file URLs" in your browser's extension settings, you can highlight and save text on local PDF documents and offline files opened in your browser.

Are there any AI or online sync features?

Currently, the extension is fully lightweight, simple, and offline-first with zero AI bloat and zero background servers. It's built purely to let you highlight, organize, and search your reading with absolute speed.
